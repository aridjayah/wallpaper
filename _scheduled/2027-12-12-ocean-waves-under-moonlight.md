Night ocean landscapes highlight simplicity and motion. The limited light sources create focus. Reflections add subtle detail. The composition feels deep and immersive. This makes it visually calming.

Using a moonlit ocean scene can reduce eye strain. The darker tones are comfortable at night. It also promotes relaxation. This improves overall experience. The mood is quiet and serene.
