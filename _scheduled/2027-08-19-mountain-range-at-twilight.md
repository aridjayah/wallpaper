Twilight mountain landscapes emphasize contrast and mood. The dark shapes create structure. Soft sky gradients add depth. The composition feels balanced and calm. This results in a soothing visual.

Using a twilight mountain scene can reduce visual strain. The darker tones are comfortable. It also creates a peaceful atmosphere. This enhances focus. The overall mood is calm and deep.
