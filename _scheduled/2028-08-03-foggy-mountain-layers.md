Layers of mountains covered in fog create a sense of depth and mystery. Each layer fades gradually into the distance. Soft tones make the composition smooth and balanced. The atmosphere feels calm and quiet. It invites contemplation.

Layered landscapes are visually pleasing due to their natural gradients. The fading effect creates perspective. Subtle variations in tone add interest. The fog softens harsh edges. This results in a gentle visual experience.

Choosing a layered mountain scene can enhance focus. The soft transitions reduce eye strain. It also creates a calming environment. This is ideal for long hours of use. The overall feeling is peaceful and deep.
