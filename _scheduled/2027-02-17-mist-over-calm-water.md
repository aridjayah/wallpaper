Misty water landscapes emphasize softness and depth. The fading layers create perspective. Light interacts subtly with fog. The composition feels smooth and balanced. This makes it visually calming.

Choosing a mist over water scene can reduce stress. The soft tones are easy on the eyes. It also creates a peaceful environment. This improves focus. The overall mood is gentle and tranquil.
