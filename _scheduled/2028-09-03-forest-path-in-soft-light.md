A narrow path winds through a dense forest illuminated by soft, filtered sunlight. Leaves glow gently as light passes through them. The scene feels enclosed yet welcoming. Subtle shadows create depth without disrupting the calm. Every element contributes to a peaceful and balanced environment.

The path itself draws the viewer forward, adding a sense of movement and direction. Textures of soil, roots, and foliage create visual interest. Light and shadow interact gently along the ground. The composition feels natural and immersive. It invites a quiet exploration through the forest.

In the distance, the path fades into softer light, enhancing the sense of depth. Colors remain rich yet controlled. The sky is barely visible through the canopy. The entire scene feels calm and grounded. It becomes a gentle journey through light and nature.
