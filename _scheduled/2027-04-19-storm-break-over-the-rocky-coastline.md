A dramatic rocky coastline stands beneath a clearing storm as heavy clouds begin to part above the ocean. Waves crash violently against jagged cliffs, sending sprays of white foam into the air. Patches of light break through the clouds, illuminating the dark sea in shifting patterns. The wind feels strong and charged with raw energy. Every detail captures the power of nature in motion.

The storm clouds slowly drift apart, revealing streaks of pale blue sky above the horizon. Sunlight reflects sharply across the restless water, creating flashes of silver and deep navy. The cliffs remain dark and imposing against the changing sky. Such scenery inspires awe and respect for the ocean’s force. It feels intense and alive.

As the storm continues to fade, the coastline becomes brighter and more defined. The waves gradually calm, though still powerful against the rocks. Every shift of light transforms the atmosphere. The scene settles into a dramatic calm. It is a breathtaking portrait of coastal transformation.
