Plains landscapes emphasize openness and simplicity. The lack of clutter enhances focus. Subtle gradients in the sky add depth. Light spreads evenly across the scene. This results in a clean and balanced composition.

Using a plains sky scene can reduce visual stress. The warm tones create comfort. It also provides a sense of space. This improves concentration. The overall mood is soft and relaxing.
