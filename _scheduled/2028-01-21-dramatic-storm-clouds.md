Storm landscapes are often associated with strength and transformation. The shifting clouds create movement and tension. Light breaking through clouds adds a striking effect. These elements make the image feel alive. It appeals to those who enjoy bold visuals.

Choosing a dramatic sky scene can energize a digital space. It creates a strong visual statement without clutter. The contrast helps highlight icons and content. It also adds a sense of depth and atmosphere. The overall effect is bold and captivating.
