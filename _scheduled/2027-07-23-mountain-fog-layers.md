Thick fog covering mountain layers creates a mysterious and deep landscape. Each layer fades into the distance. The tones remain soft and muted. Light interacts gently with the fog. It feels calm and immersive.

Fog mountain landscapes emphasize depth and perspective. The gradual fading creates visual interest. Soft lighting reduces harsh contrast. The composition feels smooth and balanced. This results in a calming visual.

Choosing a fog layer scene can improve focus. The gentle visuals reduce distraction. It also creates a quiet atmosphere. This enhances productivity. The overall mood is peaceful and deep.
