Layered mountains in smooth gradient tones create a modern and minimal landscape. Each layer fades gently into the next. The colors remain soft and controlled. The composition feels structured and clean. It highlights design simplicity.

Gradients add depth without complexity or texture. The layering effect creates visual rhythm. Soft transitions keep the scene relaxing. Each element feels intentional and balanced. The environment feels calm and refined.

Modern landscapes often symbolize clarity and design. Soft gradients bring a contemporary aesthetic. The simplicity enhances focus and calmness. It creates a stylish visual atmosphere. The scene feels sleek and minimal.
