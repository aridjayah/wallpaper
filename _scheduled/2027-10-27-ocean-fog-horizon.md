A vast ocean disappears into a thick layer of fog that blends seamlessly with the sky. The horizon becomes nearly invisible, creating a sense of endless space. Light spreads evenly, producing soft and muted tones. The atmosphere feels calm, minimal, and slightly mysterious. Every element contributes to a quiet and immersive composition.

The water surface remains smooth with only subtle ripples breaking the stillness. These small movements add texture without disrupting the calm. The fog softens all distant elements, removing sharp details. The composition feels open and uncluttered. It creates a peaceful and abstract visual experience.

As the fog thickens, the distinction between sea and sky fades completely. Colors remain soft and consistent throughout. The scene feels almost weightless and undefined. Each element supports a minimal and tranquil mood. It becomes a visual expression of silence and infinity.
