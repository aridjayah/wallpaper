A tall waterfall cascades gently down layered rock formations into a calm pool below. The flowing water appears soft and continuous, almost like a veil drifting downward. Light catches the falling stream, creating subtle highlights and a sense of motion. Surrounding rocks and greenery frame the scene naturally. The atmosphere feels refreshing and quietly powerful.

Mist rises near the base, adding a soft haze that blends the elements together. The pool reflects parts of the waterfall, creating a sense of depth and symmetry. Small details in the rocks and foliage add texture without distraction. The balance between movement and stillness remains consistent. It creates an immersive and calming visual flow.

In the background, the landscape fades gently into softer tones, enhancing the sense of depth. Colors remain cool and cohesive throughout the scene. The composition feels balanced from top to bottom. Each element supports a sense of natural harmony. It becomes a peaceful moment shaped by water and time.
