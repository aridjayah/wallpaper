Steep cliffs rise along a calm fjord, reflected clearly in still water below. The surface acts like a mirror, doubling the landscape. Light falls gently across both rock and water, creating a cohesive look. The atmosphere feels quiet and deeply serene. Every element contributes to a balanced and immersive composition.

The cliffs show layered textures and subtle color variations. These details add richness without overwhelming the scene. The reflection remains smooth with minimal distortion. The composition feels symmetrical and stable. It creates a strong sense of calm and clarity.

In the distance, the fjord narrows and fades into soft haze. The sky remains muted, complementing the tones below. Colors stay cohesive and restrained throughout. The entire scene feels still and expansive. It becomes a peaceful moment shaped by reflection and scale.
