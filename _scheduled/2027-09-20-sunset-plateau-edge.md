As the sun lowers, shadows stretch across the plateau surface. Colors deepen into rich tones of orange and red. The sky remains wide and continuous. The atmosphere feels calm and reflective. Every detail enhances the sense of space.

As twilight approaches, the colors fade into softer shades. The plateau becomes darker in silhouette. The horizon blends into the sky. The environment grows quiet and still. A peaceful calm settles across the view.
